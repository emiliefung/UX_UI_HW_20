    CASE STUDY OF

    TORONTO
    HUMANE
    SOCIETY

    Background

    The purpose of this group project was to redesign an existing nonprofit organization website. As a group, we selected a nonprofit organization that needs the most help with their UI Design. Our team was most passionate about the wellbeings of animals and a pet owner’s experience in pet adoption. We decided to redesign the Toronto Humane Society website to improve the pet adoption experience.

    Problem

    Those who visit the Toronto Humane Society in hopes of adopting a new pet often aren’t completely satisfied with their overall adoption experience. Many pet owners, who have adopted in the past, want a better adoption experience. This includes saving time with forms and interviews, and spending more time with the pet of interest.

    Solution

    The redesign of the Toronto Humane Society website brings additional features and services that improve the pet adoption process. By providing reliable adoption information with fast online services including adoption forms, interviews and secured payment, wait times at the shelter are greatly reduced. The user only needs to go to the shelter to meet their new pet.
